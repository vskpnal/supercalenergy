Privacy Policy

SuperCAL - AI Tracker for Food Calories & Exercises ("SuperCAL", "we", "us", "our") provides tools to log meals, estimate calories, track exercise and view analytics. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your data when you use the SuperCAL mobile app and related services ("App" or "Service").

SuperCAL uses AI services (such as ChatGPT or similar third-party AI providers) to analyze some of your data and generate suggestions. These AI models are not owned by us. Where applicable, certain data may be securely sent to these third-party AI providers so they can process it on our behalf.

1. Data We Collect

1.1 Account & Profile

  • Email address
  • Name
  • Age
  • Gender
  • Height and weight
  • Activity level
  • Goals (e.g., weight, calorie, or macro goals)

1.2 App Data & Usage

  • App interactions and screens you visit
  • Feature usage and in-app preferences
  • Aggregated analytics about how often certain features are used

1.3 Nutrition & Health Inputs

  • Meals you log and associated food items
  • Calories, macronutrients and other nutritional details you enter or confirm
  • Exercise sessions, their duration/intensity, and timestamps

1.4 Images

  • Photos you upload or capture for food scanning
  • Images may be processed locally on your device and/or uploaded to cloud storage (e.g., Firebase Storage) if you enable cloud backup / sync

1.5 Activity Recognition

  • Activity labels (e.g., walking, running, still)
  • Timestamps and confidence levels when you enable Activity Recognition
  • We do not collect GPS/location data as part of activity recognition unless you explicitly enable separate location features

1.6 Notifications

  • Settings and preferences for reminders and scheduled notifications (e.g., meal reminders, hydration reminders, goal prompts)

1.7 Crash & Diagnostic Data

  • Anonymized crash logs
  • Diagnostic reports used to improve app stability and performance

2. Permissions Used (Android / iOS)

  • ACTIVITY_RECOGNITION / Motion
    Android: android.permission.ACTIVITY_RECOGNITION iOS: Motion / activity permissions
    Used to detect physical activities for automatic exercise detection and calorie adjustments. Requested at runtime with an explanation.
  • Camera / Photo Library To take or select photos for food scanning. Photos are processed locally and uploaded only if you enable cloud sync/backup.
  • Storage / Files Access To read images you choose to upload and to cache processed results.
  • Notifications Android: POST_NOTIFICATIONS (and platform equivalents). Used to send meal, hydration, and goal reminders according to your settings.
  • Internet / Network Access Required for cloud sync, authentication, and optional online AI features (including access to third-party AI providers).
  • Optional: Microphone Only if you explicitly enable any voice input features (if available). Not enabled by default.

3. How We Use Data

  • Provide core features: calculate calories, macronutrients, and present analytics.
  • Personalize your goals, recommendations, and suggested intakes.
  • Enable reminders, syncing, and cloud backup (only if you enable these features).
  • Improve app functionality using aggregated analytics (opt-in for analytics may be offered where required).
  • Process certain inputs using third-party AI systems (e.g., ChatGPT or similar) to generate nutritional insights, summaries, or explanations.

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

  • Cloud Providers Images and backups may be stored on cloud services (for example, Firebase Storage/Firestore) when you enable sync or backup.
  • Analytics & Crash Reporting Aggregated and/or anonymized usage data may be collected to understand how the app is used, fix issues, and improve performance (opt-in where required).
  • Third-Party AI Providers When you use AI-based features, some data (for example text descriptions or nutrition context, and sometimes reduced/processed image data or metadata) may be sent to external AI services such as ChatGPT or similar. These AI models are provided and operated by third parties; they are not owned or controlled by us. We use them under contract and with reasonable safeguards so they can process data on our behalf.

We do not sell personal data. We do not share personally identifiable data with third parties except:

  • With your consent, or
  • When required by law, or
  • With service providers under contract who help us run the app (cloud hosting, analytics, AI processing), subject to confidentiality and data protection obligations.

5. Retention

  • Account/profile data and logs are retained for as long as you maintain an account.
  • We may retain some records as required by law or for legitimate business purposes (e.g., security logs, audit trails).

When you delete your account or request deletion, we aim to remove your profile and user-specific data from our servers and backups within a reasonable timeframe, subject to technical limitations and legal requirements. Details are in the Account & Data Deletion section at the end of this page.

6. Security

We use standard industry practices to protect your data, such as encryption in transit and access controls. However, no system is perfectly secure. In the event of a data breach, we will take reasonable steps to notify affected users and any relevant authorities as required by applicable law.

7. Children

The app is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us so we can delete it.

8. Your Controls

  • Revoke or grant permissions (camera, activity recognition, notifications, microphone, etc.) via your device Settings.
  • Disable activity detection or background tracking inside the app if those options are available.
  • Turn off cloud backup / sync in Settings if you prefer to keep data local to your device.
  • Request account deletion or data deletion by contacting support (details below) or using in-app deletion if available.

Account & Data Deletion

You can delete your account and associated data using one of the methods below.

In-App Deletion

  1. Open the SuperCAL app.
  2. Go to Settings > Account (or similar section if labeled differently).
  3. Select Send Feedback and follow the on-screen confirmation steps.

This will schedule deletion of your account profile and user-specific data from our servers. Data may continue to exist in backups for a limited period until those backups are rotated or overwritten.

Request Deletion via Email

You may also request account deletion by emailing us at vskpnal@gmail.com from the email address linked to your account. Please use the subject line:

Delete my SuperCAL account

and include a brief confirmation that you want your account deleted. We may ask for simple verification to ensure the request is legitimate and then process deletion within a reasonable timeframe.
